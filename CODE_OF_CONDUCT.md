# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be respectful, constructive and collaborative.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different experience levels and viewpoints.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on the work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats or personal attacks.
- Dismissive or insulting comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions or block participants who do not follow this code of conduct.
